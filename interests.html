Theater

I really enjoy doing theater. I am part of the drama productions that are
at San Rafael High School and the drama club (who is now run by Andrew
Herold and Austin Graff). You should totaly come check out our productions,
they are all written by these amazing people! If I'm not up there on stage
performing you can bet your behind I'm helping out behind the scenes.
Theater is a fantastic way to meet new people and an amazing medium to
express yourself. It is unbelievably fun. I am not a good singer by any means,
but being part of ensemble (the people who dance and sing and aren't the
main characters) is an incredible experience and an absolute blast.
Though it's a lot, and I mean a LOT, of work it's a great way to
exercise and enjoy yourself at the same time.

BONUS: I get to be with friends.
